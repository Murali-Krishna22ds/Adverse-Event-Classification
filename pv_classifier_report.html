Threshold Optimisation Strategy

The operating threshold of 0.55 was selected by walking the precision-recall curve and choosing the highest threshold that satisfies a minimum recall of 90% on the serious (positive) class. In patient safety workflows, false negatives — missing a serious adverse event — carry a far greater regulatory and clinical cost than false positives. This threshold achieves:

  • Recall 91.2% on serious cases — fewer than 9% of serious events are missed
  • Precision 98.7% — minimal unnecessary escalations
  • F1 0.948 — strong overall harmonic balance
